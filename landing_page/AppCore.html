Features

Every ASO tool you need

From keyword research to review management - AppCore covers the full ASO workflow.

Keyword Research

Discover high-volume, low-competition keywords for your category. Get search volume scores, difficulty ratings, and trend data for 180+ countries.

Rank Tracking

Monitor your keyword positions daily across the App Store and Google Play. Get instant alerts when you move up or drop out of the top results.

Metadata Optimizer

AI-assisted title, subtitle, and description suggestions. See keyword density, character counts, and store guidelines in real-time as you write.

Competitor Analysis

Track competitors' keyword strategies, ratings, reviews, and metadata changes over time. Benchmark your store performance side by side.

Review Management

Aggregate and analyze reviews from all storefronts. Detect sentiment trends, surface critical feedback, and respond directly from the dashboard.

A/B Testing

Run icon, screenshot, and description experiments directly on the App Store. Measure conversion lift with statistical significance reporting.
