FAQ

Pricing questions

Is the Starter plan really free forever?
Yes. No credit card required, no trial expiry. The Starter plan is permanently free for one app with up to 50 tracked keywords. You upgrade only when you need more.
How does the Pro plan "per app" pricing work?
You pay €49/month per app you connect on Pro. If you have three apps, it's €147/month. There's no cap on the number of apps — just multiply by the number you need. Annual billing saves 20% across all apps.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. If you're on a monthly plan, your Pro features remain active until the end of the billing period. Annual plans are refunded on a pro-rata basis within the first 30 days.
Do you offer a free trial of the Pro plan?
We don't offer a time-limited trial — instead, the Starter plan gives you a real, permanent free tier to try AppCore at no cost. If you want to test Pro features, reach out and we'll set up a demo or a 14-day extended trial for qualifying teams.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and SEPA direct debit. Agency and enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.
