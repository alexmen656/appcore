New Improved

Competitor Intelligence & AI Review Summaries

This release brings a completely rethought Competitor Intelligence module, plus AI-generated weekly summaries for your review inbox — so you can understand user sentiment at a glance without reading hundreds of reviews.

  • New Competitor Intelligence dashboard: metadata change history, keyword movement, and review trends for any tracked app
  • AI review summaries — weekly digest of your top themes, complaints, and feature requests generated by Claude
  • Keyword gap report: automatically highlights keywords your competitors rank for that you don't
  • Rank tracking page redesign — faster to navigate, cleaner sparklines, sortable by movement
  • Metadata editor now shows keyword density alongside character counts

v1.7.0
New Fixed

Autonomous Mode & Dark Theme

Autonomous Mode lets AppCore take proactive actions on your behalf — flagging keyword opportunities, suggesting metadata edits, and drafting review responses — all surfaced in a single action queue you approve with one click.

  • Autonomous Mode: AI-powered action queue for proactive ASO recommendations
  • Dark theme is now the default across all pages and emails
  • Slack integration for rank alerts and autonomous action notifications
  • Weekly report emails redesigned — mobile-optimized, cleaner layout
  • Fixed an issue where Google Play keyword ranks were delayed by up to 36 hours
  • Fixed: adding an app via its Play Store URL now correctly identifies the package name

A/B testing is now available to all Pro users. Run experiments on icons, screenshots, and descriptions with automatic statistical significance detection. We also shipped a major landing page redesign.

  • Real screenshot integration in the hero — shows your actual app dashboard
  • Country selector on keyword search — now defaults to your app's primary market
  • Onboarding flow reduced from 6 steps to 3
  • Keyword difficulty scores recalibrated — now more accurate for niche categories

Managing multiple apps is now much faster. The new multi-app overview shows all your apps and their KPIs at a glance, and bulk keyword import via CSV cuts setup time for new apps from hours to minutes.

New

Review Management & Sentiment Analysis

The review inbox launched in this release, bringing all App Store and Google Play reviews into a single place with AI-powered sentiment categorization and topic clustering.
